Summary:
Vincenzo Cassano, Korean-Italian mob lawyer and consigliere of the Cassano family, returns to his homeland to search for a place to stash the mob's assets and find further money-laundering business opportunities. However, with the corrupt Babel Corporate Group and its cronies dead set on gentrifying any business opportunities, Vincenzo is forced to band with the locals of a small town in order to take the fight against the tyranny of Babel and secure the Cassano family's foothold, unwittingly becoming a warrior of justice in the process.
